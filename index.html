Akash Kumar

I am a designer, artist and researcher based in the Netherlands. I work with materials, trying an experiment with them in different contexts, and blurring the boundary. Alumni of the National Institute of Design Ahmedabad India. I have started my MA in Industrial Design at the Royal Academy of Art The Hague.

My work comprises problem-solving, sustainability, and responsible design which is an integral part of my design philosophy. Being a designer I am always looking out in nature for inspiration and material innovation that can solve real-life problems. I like to challenge myself every day, push boundaries to achieve my goal, and never stop learning.
